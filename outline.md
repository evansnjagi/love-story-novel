# Story outline

## Title
Lost lover novel

## Genre

Romance/ Emotional drama/ Tragedy

## Core premise

A young man, who once had nothing, unexpectedly finds the love of his life at his lowest point. But just as he begun to rebuild the world around her, she disapers when he needed her the most, forcing him into a painful journey of love, loss, and self-discovery.


## Themes

- Love found unexpectedly.
- The pain of losing your world(a lover).
- What we remember Vs what is true.
- Emotional dependancies and real attachments.
- The search of love in uncertainities.
- Growth out of realization.
- Hope is everything.
- Zero becomes lazy and one is never enough.
