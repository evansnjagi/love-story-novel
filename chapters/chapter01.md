# The Empty Life

Daev awake to the melodic songs of birds drifting through his window. In a distance, their faint chirping sounded almost festive, as though they were celebrating a holiday of their own.

Without giving it much thought, he lazily opened his eyes.
"Oh my gosh, what time is it?"

He kicked off the blanket and rushed towards his reading table, where his alarm clock lay beside a pile of scattered books. After a quick glance at the time, he let out a quiet breath of relief. He was still on time. He had simply panicked.

For a moment, he stood silently in the cold room. More than anything, Daev wanted to move out and start his own life, a life without strict rules, one he could finally do whatever he wanted. 

He looked at his bed, he had laid on it for more than 10 years. Today is the day he is going to leave it. 

"If I leave here, I will never come back again. I am tired of living here broke. I want to make my own money," Daev said to himself reassuaringly. Recently, he had finished his KCSE exams, after four years of serious studies, he's free.
