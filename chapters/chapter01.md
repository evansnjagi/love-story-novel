# The empty life
---

Daev awoke to birdsong drifting through his window, soft and unhurried, as if the world outside was not aware that today was the day everything would change. He lazily open one of his eyes, still half-wrapped in the warmth of the sleep. His mind snapped awake. His eyes shot wide open. "Oh my gosh, what time is it?" He kicked off his blanket and rushed to his reading table, where his alarm clock lay beside a pile of scattered books. He glanced at the time and sighed with relief. "Thank God I am still on time, how can I be late?". He was still on time. He had simply panicked.
