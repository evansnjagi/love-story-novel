# Main characters

## 1. The young man(protagonist)

- **Name** - Daev
- **Background** -  Daev starts life with nothing. He has faced and experienced real life struggles, loneliness, and emotional emptiness before meeting the love of his life
- **Personalities**
    - Quet
    - Observant
    - Hardworking
    - Caring
    - Hopeful
    - Supportive
    - Reliable
    - Deep lover
    - Soft hearted
    - Emotional unstable

### Motivation

Make money, be rich, have a stable life, and atleast have a lover to support his endovours.

### Internal conflict

He struggles to hold on to hope that he'll finally get someone better and accepting that she may never return in his life.

- **Hidden truths(by Daev)**
    - Daev quited his first job.
    - He was broke and couldn't support her financially.
    - Emotionally he was weak, drained from all levels of life, even from his family.
    - He hoped she would get a better lover, who would take better care of her.
    - Conteplated a broke up plan unknown to her.

## The woman(mystery)

- **Name** - Evda
- **Background** - Out of the blue, she appear in Daevs life. At his lowest, she brings wormth, hapiness, hope and emotional healing. Indeed God allowed her in his life for a reason.
- **Personality**
    - Calm
    - Kind
    - Caring
    - Loving
    - Emotionally intelligent
    - Protective
    - Hardworking
- **Relationship with Daev** - Destiny brought them together and their connection has grown stronger. At first they are lover, she almost became the gentlemans wife, unfortunately worst happened.
- **Mystery element** - Were they both in love? For Daev, they broke up out of love. For Evda, is something else. 
- **Emotional impact** - Her leaving becomes the emotional wound that drives this entire story.
